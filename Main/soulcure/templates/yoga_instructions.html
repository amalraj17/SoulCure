Instructions

Follow these instructions to ensure accurate yoga pose detection:

Positioning: Make sure you are in a well-lit area with your full body visible to the camera.

Camera Placement: Place the camera at a suitable angle to capture your entire body. Ensure that the camera is stable and doesn't move during the session.

Clothing: Wear comfortable clothing that allows your body to move freely. Bright or contrasting colors can help.

Background: Use a plain and uncluttered background to make it easier for the detection system to focus on your body.

Please note that the video window will open separately. To close the video window, press the "Escape" button on your keyboard.

These instructions will help ensure accurate yoga pose detection during your practice. Enjoy your session!
